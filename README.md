# Lecture: Laser Ablation in Dentistry – Moving Beyond the Scalpel

Welcome to the official repository for the academic lecture and operational guidelines exploring how laser ablation technology is revolutionizing modern oral healthcare. Driven by the core mission of **VirusTC** to phase out mechanical scalpels in medicine, this material details the replacement of traditional surgical tools with precise, light-based energy systems.
